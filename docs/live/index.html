About

In our rapidly moving world, people are moving faster than ever before. The cars on our roads are more advanced than they ever have been and now we need a traffic management system to match. Enter ITCS â€“ the innovative new product which will revolutionise the way we drive, making traffic on our roads faster, safer and more fuel-efficient using clever mesh-network technology. Our state of the art mesh network topology combined with a clever communications and sensor will enable vehicles on the road to communicate with each other, much in the same way as a driver might have communicated with another in the past, except for the fact that these controllers never make mistakes and always knows the right decision to make.

Meet Our Staff

Meet the (slightly unfortunate and sleep deprived) real faces behind ICMesh

Arthur Stemmer

Lead Hardware Developer

Arthur fell in love with hardware at an early stage in his life. During university, he and Michal conceived the brainchild that was to become ICMesh. Arthur is also a keen runner, recently completing a half marathon.

Piers de Basto

Sensor Sepcialist

Piers dabbles in the black art of sensors, balancing on the edge of the real and digital divide. He also works closely with the core algorithm team, where performance is the holy grail. Piers is a swimming enthusiast. He plans to traverse the English channel... by boat, but maybe swimming one day.

Haaris Mehmood

Guru/Web Developer

With over 3 years experience developing websites under his belt, Haaris operates on the cutting edge of front end web design. He also works with the sensor and simulation teams and is an all round Guru. In his free time, he enjoys tennis amongst other things.

Mohika Gupta

Head of Operations

Mohika has been instrumental in the development of ICMesh. Choosing to fight with the pen over the sword, she has ensured that developement deadlines have been strictly adhered to. She also does Jiu jitsu.

Waleed El-Geresy

Senior Software Developer

With a sharp mind and quick wit, Waleed is often spotted with a squash racket in his hands. Waleed co-ordinates all software development, inspiring others to reach new levels of performance, safety and reliability.

Michal Mysior

Comunications Specialist

Michal was one of the co-founders of ICMesh, conceiving the idea whilst frustrated with the traffic situation on his daily commute. Michal is often refered to as the "Roller King" due to his incredible rollerblading skills.

Ashish Sundar

Power Specialist

As comfortable on stage as in the hardware Lab, Ashish is the team's rock star. He is the lead drummer in the team's band and often performs live. A lover of power and heavy metal.
